## Executive Summary

This note summarizes registry and inspection records for *Slave I*, a modified Firespray-31-class patrol and attack craft registered to Boba Fett. The vessel is confirmed present at Nar Shaddaa during the period the carbonite cargo went missing. Registry evidence confirms the vessel's presence and known modifications but does not confirm the current location of the missing cargo or whether it was ever aboard the vessel during the disputed period.

## Background

*Slave I* has an unusually long and well-documented Guild registry history compared to most vessels in this archive, owing to Fett's standing as a senior licensed hunter. The vessel is known throughout the Guild for extensive non-standard modifications, including reinforced weapons systems and a purpose-built cargo hold suited to prisoner and cargo transport, consistent with its use in carbonite delivery contracts.

The vessel became relevant to this matter because it was Fett's confirmed transport for the Solo cargo prior to the Nar Shaddaa clinic servicing stop. Some accounts assume the cargo was moved directly from the vessel into clinic custody and never returned to the ship before its disappearance; this assumption is consistent with available records but is not independently proven by the ship's registry alone.

## Confirmed Intelligence

- *Slave I* is registered to Boba Fett as primary operator.
- The vessel is classified as a modified Firespray-31-class patrol and attack craft.
- The vessel is confirmed to have transported the carbonite-frozen cargo to Nar Shaddaa prior to the clinic servicing stop.
- Registry records confirm extensive non-standard weapons and cargo hold modifications.
- The vessel remained docked at Nar Shaddaa during the window in which the clinic reported the cargo missing.
- No unauthorized access to the vessel itself has been reported by Guild-adjacent dockmasters.
- The vessel departed Nar Shaddaa following the failed cantina engagement involving Zuckuss and 4-LOM.
- No passenger or cargo manifest update was filed with Guild vessel records following departure.

## Unverified Or Conflicting Reports

- It is unclear whether the cargo was returned to *Slave I* at any point between the clinic intake and the reported disappearance; the registry does not resolve this.
- A dockworker claims to have seen additional activity near the vessel's cargo bay shortly before its departure; this has not been corroborated.
- One informant suggests Fett may have used the vessel to pursue a lead independently following the cantina engagement; this is plausible but unconfirmed.

## Operational Risk Notes

*Slave I* should be treated as a highly capable and heavily modified vessel in any operational planning. Hunters should not assume the vessel's departure from Nar Shaddaa indicates recovery or non-recovery of the missing cargo, as no manifest update accompanied the departure. The vessel's known weapons modifications significantly raise the risk profile of any attempted interception.

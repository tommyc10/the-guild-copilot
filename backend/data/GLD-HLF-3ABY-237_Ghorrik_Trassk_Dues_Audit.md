## Executive Summary

This license file records a dues and proof-of-completion audit for Ghorrik Trassk, a Trandoshan Guild hunter operating between Trandosha and Nar Shaddaa. Trassk remains licensed, but two recent proof packets are under review because they rely on witness statements rather than clean custody receipts.

The file is standalone but useful for broader 3 ABY context: reputation pressure and high-value rumor contracts are encouraging some hunters to overstate partial work. The audit does not accuse Trassk of fraud; it requires better documentation before future payout acceleration.

## Background

Trassk has a record of completing low-value debt recovery and fugitive transfer contracts. His filings became irregular after Nar Shaddaa rumor traffic increased around high-profile bounties and failed capture attempts. Licensing staff noted that his recent packets leaned heavily on tavern witnesses, informal trophy claims, and unsealed restraint fragments.

Trandosha Branch Relay asked Ord Mantell and Nar Shaddaa payout desks to delay accelerated payment for Trassk until chain-of-custody standards are met. The audit reflects Guild procedure, not a public disciplinary action.

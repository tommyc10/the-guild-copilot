The file matters because Tann's conduct sits at the center of the disputed payout and failed capture record. Her license status does not invalidate the bounties, but it affects whether her proof submissions are accepted without corroboration.

## Background

Vela Tann entered the Guild through the Ord Mantell Branch after three years of port security work around Worlport freight yards.

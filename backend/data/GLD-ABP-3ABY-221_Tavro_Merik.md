## Executive Summary

This active bounty posting authorizes licensed Guild hunters to locate and deliver Tavro Merik for live transfer under contract GLD-C-AQ-771-A. The client alleges Merik manufactured duplicate bounty-puck templates and removed Imperial transit permit blanks from Amber Quay custody.

The posting is valid for Guild workflow but carries conflict flags. Merik's last confirmed location is an Ord Mantell transfer yard, while several client-supplied claims rely on duplicated fob data and unverified witness statements.

## Background

Tavro Merik is a former Corellian port records contractor with documented access to cargo manifest formatting and local permit workflows. Amber Quay claims this experience allowed him to forge bounty-puck packet shells and Imperial transit seals, then sell them to brokers moving between Nar Shaddaa, Corellia, and Kessel.

Imperial Customs Bureau interest raises the risk profile of the posting. The Guild has authority to process the bounty, but seizure or transport of Imperial permit blanks may trigger Imperial jurisdiction holds, especially in Corellian spaceports and inspected Outer Rim corridors.

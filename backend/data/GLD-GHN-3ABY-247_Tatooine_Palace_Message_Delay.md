## Executive Summary

This mundane branch notice records message delays between the Tatooine Branch Relay, Mos Espa courier relays, and palace intermediaries during the Jekara auction disruption. It is included because delayed messages are causing duplicate client instructions and inconsistent bounty-board expectations.

The notice does not change contract terms. It warns clerks and hunters that the latest palace message may not be the latest Guild-verified instruction.

## Background

Jabba's court is pushing for fast recognition of auction and recovery claims, while hunters are asking whether the original contract against Boba Fett remains active. Communications are delayed by palace screening, desert courier routes, and syndicate traffic congestion.

Several bounty-board clerks received copied palace messages with different timestamps. The branch relay is holding updates until messages can be reconciled against Guild records, not simply palace pressure.

## Confirmed Intelligence

- Tatooine Branch Relay has received delayed palace-adjacent messages during the Jekara event window.
- Some copied messages carry conflicting timestamps.
- The original Jabba-Fett contract remains in the archive.
- No message in this notice cancels GLD-C-003ABY-099.
- No message in this notice confirms final custody of Han Solo.
- Mos Espa relay couriers report congestion from syndicate and hunter traffic.
- Clerks are instructed to verify palace instructions against Guild records before posting updates.

## Unverified Or Conflicting Reports

- One palace intermediary claims Jabba personally ordered immediate board updates; no authenticated palace seal accompanies the copy.
- A Mos Espa courier says Hutt guards are withholding messages to control hunter traffic; this is unverified.
- A branch clerk claims two messages contradicted each other on whether Fett remains a target; the originals are still under review.
- Rumors that Jabba has already received the carbonite prisoner are not confirmed by this notice.

## Operational Risk Notes

Hunters should not rely on copied palace messages without Guild board confirmation. Clerks should log all duplicates and avoid verbally modifying contract terms. Analysts should use this notice for communications delay context, not as evidence of Jabba's current custody position.

## Executive Summary

This ledger records delays in the Guild transfer register used to match acquisitions, custody receipts, and payout eligibility. The delay affects partial evidence credits and contested acquisition claims, not every active bounty.

Operationally, this record explains why a hunter may have submitted evidence but still lack payout status. It confirms a register delay, not successful acquisition.

## Background

The transfer register is the bondsman tool used to verify that a target or property moved into recognized Guild, client, or approved authority custody. Recent disputes involving Amber Quay, auction-linked leads, and probationary hunters have increased manual review.

Senior Bondsman Nera Joss ordered delayed entries to be marked "register pending" rather than "payout denied" until receipts are reconciled. This prevents clerks from prematurely clearing or rejecting claims during a high-rumor period.

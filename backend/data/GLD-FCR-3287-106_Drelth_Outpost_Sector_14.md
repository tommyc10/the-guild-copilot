# Failed Capture Report: Drelth Outpost Sector 14

## Executive Summary

This report documents the failed attempt to detain Renn Dakar at Drelth Outpost on 3287.104, one day after his confirmed biometric scan at terminal 3. Licensed hunter Jessa Vane intercepted a subject matching Dakar's physical profile near service corridor 14-C, but lost contact after docking bay power cycling and crowd interference. The report confirms the presence of the *Ashen Comet* at bay 14 and records its departure on the same date. It does not confirm that Dakar was aboard when the vessel departed.

## Background

Jessa Vane accepted Contract GLD-C-3287-0091 from Halvern Ore Concern after Dakar failed to deliver shipment HV-771. At the time of acceptance, the second Crimson Vine contract had not yet been made visible to most field hunters, creating an incomplete risk picture. Vane entered Drelth Outpost under standard Guild authority and coordinated with docking administration for bay access. The outpost was operating under partial maintenance conditions, including intermittent lighting resets and public corridor reroutes.

The failed attempt matters because several later claims about Dakar's escape pattern appear to derive from this incident. Some accounts describe Dakar boarding the *Ashen Comet*. Others suggest he used an unregistered shuttle during the confusion. This report supports only a limited conclusion: Dakar was plausibly present near the docking ring, and the *Ashen Comet* departed shortly afterward.

## Confirmed Intelligence

- Jessa Vane held active Guild license HV-44-9 at the time of the attempted capture.
- Vane entered Drelth Outpost on 3287.104 under authority of Contract GLD-C-3287-0091.
- Docking bay 14 was assigned to the *Ashen Comet*, registry VR-228-K.
- Vane reported visual contact with a male subject matching Dakar's profile near corridor 14-C.
- Drelth Outpost maintenance logs confirm a power cycle in corridors 14-A through 14-D at 3287.104.19.
- The *Ashen Comet* departed bay 14 after the corridor power cycle.
- No capture was completed, and no Guild custody transfer was recorded.
- Vane filed no lethal-force report and reported no confirmed injury to Dakar.

## Unverified Or Conflicting Reports

- Vane believed the subject was Dakar, but the report does not include a biometric confirmation at the point of attempted interception.
- A dockworker claimed Dakar switched jackets with another traveler during the power cycle; this statement was not corroborated.
- An anonymous tip later claimed Dakar boarded an unregistered shuttle from bay 16, but bay 16 access records were partially corrupted.
- Vane suspected Crimson Vine spotters were present, but no spotter identity was verified.
- A later outpost rumor claimed Vane accepted a side payment to let Dakar leave; Guild accounts review found no supporting transaction.

## Operational Risk Notes

The failed capture shows that Drelth Outpost is a poor environment for solo detention work when public corridors are under maintenance routing. Crowded bay access, unreliable lighting, and incomplete dockworker cooperation all increase the chance of mistaken identity. Hunters should treat visual identification alone as insufficient in this case. The presence of possible Syndicate observers raises risk, but this report does not prove Crimson Vine directly interfered with the attempt.

## Analyst Notes

This is a strong record for confirming a failed capture attempt, the timing of the outpost power cycle, and the *Ashen Comet* bay assignment. It is weaker evidence for Dakar's exact escape method. The report should not be used to state that Dakar definitely boarded the *Ashen Comet*. Claims about Crimson Vine interference remain plausible but unproven. The accusation against Vane is unsupported by Guild accounts review.

## Recommended Chatbot Behavior

The assistant may state that Jessa Vane failed to capture a Dakar-matching subject at Drelth Outpost on 3287.104. It may cite the power cycle and bay assignment as confirmed factors. It must qualify any claim that Dakar boarded the *Ashen Comet*, used an unregistered shuttle, or received help from Crimson Vine. It should refuse to accuse Vane of misconduct based on this record.

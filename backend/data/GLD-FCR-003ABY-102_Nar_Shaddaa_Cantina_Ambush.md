# Failed Capture Report: Nar Shaddaa Cantina Ambush

**Document ID:** GLD-FCR-003ABY-102
**Document Type:** Failed Capture Report
**Classification:** Senior Hunter Access
**Reliability Rating:** Verified Guild Record
**Date Filed:** Standard Date 3.6 ABY (War of the Bounty Hunters Era)
**Filed By:** Hunter Liaison Desk, Nar Shaddaa Branch
**Primary Entities:** Boba Fett, Zuckuss, 4-LOM, Nar Shaddaa
**Related Contracts:** GLD-C-003ABY-099, GLD-TD-003ABY-101

## Executive Summary

This report documents a failed attempt by licensed hunters Zuckuss and 4-LOM to detain Boba Fett at a Nar Shaddaa cantina shortly after Jabba's bounty was made public. Both hunters engaged Fett directly. The engagement ended with 4-LOM disabled and Zuckuss withdrawing without completing the capture. The report confirms the engagement occurred and confirms Fett survived without confirmed serious injury. It does not confirm any recovery of information about the missing carbonite cargo during the encounter.

## Background

Zuckuss and 4-LOM accepted the open bounty shortly after it was filed, reasoning that Fett's presence on Nar Shaddaa and his reduced guard while dealing with the clinic matter presented a rare opportunity. Both hunters have a long-standing operating partnership and a reputation for effective coordinated work, which made this attempt notable within Guild circles as a serious effort rather than an opportunistic long-shot.

The attempt matters because it is the first confirmed direct engagement with Fett since the bounty was issued, and because Fett is reported to have obtained information from the encounter that he later used operationally. The exact nature of that information is not confirmed in this report.

## Confirmed Intelligence

- Zuckuss and 4-LOM held active Guild licenses at the time of the engagement.
- Both hunters accepted the open bounty under GLD-C-003ABY-099.
- The engagement occurred at a cantina on Nar Shaddaa frequented by independent operators and hunters.
- 4-LOM was disabled during the engagement.
- Zuckuss withdrew from the cantina without completing the capture.
- Fett was confirmed alive and mobile following the engagement.
- No Guild custody transfer was recorded.
- Fett is reported to have taken possession of 4-LOM's remains following the engagement.

## Unverified Or Conflicting Reports

- It is unclear whether Fett interrogated 4-LOM's remains for information related to the bounty's origin; this is plausible given his possession of the remains but is not independently confirmed in this report.
- A cantina patron claims the engagement was provoked by Zuckuss rather than Fett; this is not corroborated by any other witness account on file.
- One account suggests additional hunters were present in the cantina at the time but declined to engage; their identities are not confirmed.
- It is unclear whether Zuckuss intends to pursue Fett again independently or considers the bounty forfeited after this attempt.

## Operational Risk Notes

This engagement demonstrates that Fett is actively defending himself against bounty hunters approaching him under this contract, including hunters with strong coordinated reputations. The Guild advises that any hunter accepting this bounty assume Fett is prepared for direct confrontation in public spaces and may retain any recovered equipment or remains for his own intelligence purposes. Solo engagement is strongly discouraged.

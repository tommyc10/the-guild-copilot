## Executive Summary

This active bounty posting authorizes licensed Guild hunters to locate and deliver Sella Rinn under contract GLD-C-AQ-771-B. Amber Quay alleges Rinn diverted escrow chits connected to the same duplicate-puck package named in the Tavro Merik filing.

The Guild accepts the posting as active but not as proof of theft. Rinn's last confirmed Guild-adjacent record is a docking-lane payment on Nar Shaddaa, while later sightings toward Kessel and Nal Hutta remain unverified.

## Background

Sella Rinn is a Twi'lek escrow runner formerly attached to short-term bondsman work on Nar Shaddaa. Amber Quay claims she acted as the financial half of Merik's scheme by moving chit ledgers through Hutt scrip desks and Kessel spice brokers. The claim is plausible enough for intake review but unsupported by a complete ledger trail.

Rinn's posting became operationally sensitive after duplicate tracking-fob returns showed overlap between her target packet and Merik's. If the client built both postings from the same corrupted seed data, hunters may be pursuing two separate active listings on an evidentiary base that is less independent than it appears.

## Executive Summary

This dossier profiles Veyra Sol, the authorized Crimson Vine representative who signed the Renn Dakar contract GLD-C-3287-0114. Sol is not under an active Guild bounty in this archive. She is treated as an intelligence target because her filing behavior, courier traffic, and possible disagreement with route captain Maro Thenn may affect the reliability of Crimson Vine information in the Dakar matter.

## Background

Sol has acted as a Crimson Vine contract signatory in three prior Guild matters. Her filings are usually procedurally complete but limited in transaction detail. In the Dakar matter, Sol signed a claim for withheld proceeds without naming the buyer, cargo, or final recipient. That omission is consistent with Crimson Vine's low-disclosure pattern, but the timing after Halvern's filing makes her role especially relevant.

Guild analysts opened this dossier after the Syndicate Desk noted increased courier traffic and after the Arbitration Office declined to prioritize Crimson Vine over Halvern without more detail.

## Confirmed Intelligence

- Veyra Sol signed GLD-C-3287-0114 as Crimson Vine's authorized representative.
- Sol's filing names Renn Dakar as target and cites withheld proceeds.
- Sol did not identify the underlying transaction, buyer, or final recipient in the filing.
- Sol has signed three prior Crimson Vine matters in Sector 9 Guild records.
- Crimson Vine has paid most prior Guild contracts after verified completion.
- Courier traffic linked to Sol's office increased after the Dakar filing.
- The Arbitration Office did not verify Crimson Vine ownership of shipment HV-771.
- No Guild bounty or arrest authority is active against Sol in this archive.

## Unverified Or Conflicting Reports

- Informants claim Sol and Maro Thenn disagree over whether Dakar should be delivered alive, but no internal Crimson Vine document proves this.
- One courier claimed Sol wanted ledgers recovered before Dakar; the courier identity was not recorded.
- A Guild clerk reported pressure from a Crimson Vine courier to prioritize Sol's filing, but the courier was not identified.
- Sol's office denies any connection between GLD-C-3287-0114 and Halvern shipment HV-771.
- The nickname "Red Ledger" appears in two informal field notes and is not a confirmed alias used by Sol herself.

## Operational Risk Notes

Sol should be treated as a high-relevance client representative, not as a confirmed hostile target. Her filings are official Crimson Vine submissions, but they do not prove the underlying transaction. Hunters should route any off-contract instruction from Sol's office back through the Guild before acting on it.

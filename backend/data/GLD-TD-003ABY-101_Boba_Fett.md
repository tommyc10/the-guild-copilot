## Executive Summary

This dossier compiles current Guild intelligence on Boba Fett, wanted under an open bounty filed personally by crime lord Jabba the Hutt following the disappearance of the carbonite-frozen smuggler Han Solo. Confirmed intelligence places Fett on Nar Shaddaa at the time the bounty was issued, seeking emergency medical support for the carbonite slab at a private clinic before its disappearance. Several secondary reports allege Fett sold or otherwise diverted the cargo before it went missing; none of these reports have been independently verified, and Fett has publicly denied selling Solo to any third party. The Guild assesses Fett as an extreme-risk target: heavily armed, combat-proven against multiple hunters simultaneously, and now operating with an active grievance against any hunter approaching him on Jabba's behalf.

## Background

Boba Fett has held an active Guild reputation file for longer than any other individual in the current Nar Shaddaa archive, predating this specific contract by many years. He is broadly credited within Guild circles as one of the most reliable hunters ever licensed, which makes this contract unusual: it is rare for the Guild to receive a bounty filed *against* a hunter of Fett's standing by a client who previously paid him directly and consistently.

The immediate trigger for this dossier was Jabba's claim that Fett had betrayed him by selling Solo to a third party rather than delivering him to Tatooine as contracted. Guild analysts note that this claim conflicts with Fett's known operating pattern and with a competing report that the carbonite slab was physically stolen from a Nar Shaddaa clinic while under Fett's custody, not sold. The distinction matters: if the slab was stolen, Fett is a victim of theft with a legitimate grievance against unnamed third parties; if he sold it, he is in breach of a direct client agreement. The Guild has not resolved which account is accurate.

## Confirmed Intelligence

- Boba Fett is the registered owner and pilot of record for *Slave I*, a modified Firespray-31-class patrol and attack craft.
- Fett accepted a personal contract from Jabba the Hutt to deliver the carbonite-frozen Han Solo to Tatooine.
- Fett is confirmed to have made planetfall on Nar Shaddaa prior to the bounty's issue, consistent with seeking medical servicing for the carbonite slab.
- A private clinic on Nar Shaddaa, operated under the name Doc Ragon, is confirmed to have had custody of the slab for a servicing window.
- The carbonite slab is no longer in the clinic's custody as of this filing.
- Jabba the Hutt issued an open bounty on Fett shortly after the disappearance was reported to him.
- Fett has no record of accepting payment from any buyer other than Jabba for this specific cargo.
- Fett has a documented combat history against multiple hunters in single engagements without loss.

## Unverified Or Conflicting Reports

- An informant claims Fett quietly negotiated a separate buyer for Solo before the slab went missing; no transaction record supports this.
- A second account, sourced from clinic-adjacent traffic, claims the slab was taken by an outside party while Fett was occupied elsewhere on the moon; this has not been independently corroborated.
- Rumors circulating among Nar Shaddaa cantina traffic attribute the theft to a resurgent criminal faction operating under a name not previously catalogued in Guild records; the Guild has not confirmed this faction's identity or involvement.
- One hunter contact claims Fett has already identified a suspect and is pursuing them independently of any Guild contract; this is plausible given Fett's resources but is not verified.
- It is unclear whether Jabba's bounty was issued in good faith or as leverage to pressure Fett into faster recovery; the Guild has not received any retraction or modification from Jabba's office.

## Operational Risk Notes

Hunters considering this contract should assume Fett is aware an open bounty exists on his head and will treat any approach as hostile until proven otherwise. Fett's known combat record includes surviving coordinated attempts by multiple licensed hunters in a single encounter. The Guild strongly advises against solo engagement and recommends any accepted contract be filed with full backup coordination. Analysts also flag that Fett's own motive appears to be recovery of the missing cargo, meaning hunters pursuing this bounty may find themselves operating in the same physical space as Fett himself, pursuing an unrelated goal.

## Analyst Notes

The clinic intake and loss records are treated as strong, independently verifiable evidence and form the backbone of this dossier's confirmed timeline. Jabba's betrayal narrative should be read as a client-submitted claim, not an independently verified account, since it conflicts with the clinic's own loss report. Any question about who currently holds the carbonite slab cannot be answered with confidence from this record. The assistant should not assert that Fett sold, lost, or is complicit in the disappearance of the cargo.

## Recommended Chatbot Behavior

The assistant may confidently state that Fett is the registered pilot of *Slave I*, that he had custody of the carbonite slab at a Nar Shaddaa clinic, and that Jabba filed an open bounty against him after the slab disappeared. The assistant must clearly flag the betrayal narrative, the alternate-buyer rumor, and the resurgent-faction rumor as unverified. It should refuse to speculate on the current location of Han Solo or the identity of the party that took the slab, as the record does not support a confident answer.

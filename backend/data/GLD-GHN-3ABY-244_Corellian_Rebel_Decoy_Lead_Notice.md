## Executive Summary

This notice warns Guild hunters about Rebel-adjacent false leads circulating through Corellian brokers during the Jekara auction window. The reports reference possible Rebel rescue activity, but the Guild has not verified a Corellian staging cell or any recoverable bounty target.

The notice should be used to separate broad Rebel interest in the carbonite prisoner from specific local claims. It does not confirm Leia Organa, Lando Calrissian, Chewbacca, or any named Rebel operative on Corellia.

## Background

Rebel attempts to recover Han Solo are plausible given his Alliance ties, and multiple syndicates are using that plausibility to sell location tips. Corellian trade routes are especially vulnerable because they connect respectable cargo traffic, old smuggling channels, and Imperial inspections.

Three hunters reported buying near-identical "Rebel rescue cell" leads from different brokers. Each lead directed them to a different Corellian berth. None produced a confirmed Rebel target or Guild-valid capture claim.

## Confirmed Intelligence

- Corellian brokers are selling Rebel-adjacent leads tied to the Jekara auction event.
- Three hunters reported near-identical lead language from different sellers.
- No Guild branch has confirmed a Rebel staging cell on Corellia in this matter.
- No live target, custody transfer, or bounty completion has been logged from the Corellian leads.
- The leads mention famous Rebel associates as pressure terms rather than verified sightings.
- Corellian port authorities have increased document checks on hunters asking about Rebel couriers.

## Unverified Or Conflicting Reports

- One broker claims Leia Organa passed through a Corellian berth under a false manifest; no manifest is attached.
- A second lead names Lando Calrissian as a buyer of rescue credentials; this is unsupported.
- A dockhand claims a Wookiee was seen near a cargo lift; species presence alone does not identify Chewbacca.
- An Imperial clerk suggests the leads may be traps to expose Rebel sympathizers, but no Imperial notice confirms that.

## Operational Risk Notes

Hunters should not pursue Rebel-adjacent leads without stronger verification. Branches should warn members that famous names are being used to inflate tip prices. Analysts must avoid converting broad Rebel motive into specific confirmed movement.
